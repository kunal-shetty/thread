Frequently asked

How is Thread different from Google Docs?
Thread is purpose-built for professional writers. While Google Docs offers great basic collaboration, Thread adds an AI writing assistant, editorial workflow states, a knowledge base, research panel, and profession-specific modes — all in one integrated workspace designed for writing teams.
Is my data private and secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Each organization's data is fully isolated — no shared tables for content. We're pursuing SOC 2 Type II certification and are GDPR and CCPA compliant.
Can I import my existing documents?
Yes — Thread supports import from Google Docs and Notion, with Word (.docx) import also available. We've designed the import flow to preserve formatting and comments so your team can switch without losing work.
What AI model powers the writing assistant?
The AI assistant is powered by Anthropic's Claude — one of the most capable writing models available. The AI has full context of your document, style guide, and knowledge base, making suggestions far more relevant than generic AI tools.
Is there a mobile app?
iOS and Android apps are on the roadmap for v2.0. The web app is fully responsive and works great on mobile browsers in the meantime, with touch-optimized interactions throughout.
